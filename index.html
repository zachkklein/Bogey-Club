Bogey Club

About us

The Bogey Club is more than just a group of people, it is a city-wide golfing community. The Bogey Club pairs golfers from throughout Los Angeles and host tournaments, fun outings, and much more. We currently consist of over 350 members and are rapidly growing. Even though the Bogey Golf Club is based in Los Angeles, we are looking to grow into a nation wide brand and expand the game of golf throughout all of America.

How to Join

Membership to the Bogey Club is open to all SCGA golfers with an official handicap. New Members must pay a fee of $40 to join and then $15 per month which pays for tournaments and events. Membership to the Bogey Club gets members access to a wide range of golf instructors, courses, and much more.

Current Members

Throughout the over 350 members of the Bogey Club, some of our famed members are Rick Shiels, Eric Anders Lang, JR Smith, Collin Morikawa, and countless other incredible people and golfers.

Upcoming Events

Every 2 weeks, the Bogey Club holds a new event. The events can be anyhting from tournaments, pro AM match ups, match play, skins games, glow in the dark golf, and so much more that is paid for by the membership fees. All of Bogey Golf Club events allow the member and one guest. Tee times are first come first serve and can be reserved through our app. At each event there is exclusive Bogey Golf merchandise for members to purchase and will only for sale at the event.

Our Courses

All of our Bogey Club events are held at course in Los Angeles. This includes public courses, private courses, and country clubs. Bogey Club members get a discount at all Los Angeles City Golf Courses.
